Bio

After spending years as a professional bassist, Will Martin has released music under the moniker Lliam. His debut Ep "Heart Moves" was released in July 2018, just days after getting off the road playing major tours across the nation. Lliam has several more releases set for early 2019, along with live shows in his hometown of Nashville, Tennessee, and beyond. Stay tuned.
